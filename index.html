Dr. Aditya Nigam received his Masters (M.Tech) and Doctoral (Ph.D) degrees from Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur in 2009 and 2014 respectively. Presentely, he is working as an Associate Professor at IIT Mandi, HP in the School of Computing and Electrical Engineering (SCEE). He joined this School during August 2014 as a Teaching Fellow. He is passionate about teaching and most of his last 6 years his teaching is related to Deep Learning and its applications, covering concepts starting from basic to advanced level.

His research interests are Deep Learning, Biometrics, Medical Image Processing & its Analysis, Image Processing, Computer Vision & Machine Learning.
